The Floating Banana Tower 🍌

Our most ambitious project began with a simple question: "What if gravity was more of a suggestion?"

The Floating Banana Tower is a 72-floor experimental skyscraper inspired by the natural elegance of a slightly overripe banana. Instead of straight lines, the tower bends gracefully toward the sun, creating a silhouette that scientists describe as "architecturally suspicious but emotionally inspiring."

Inside the building you will find:

🌿 Vertical forests growing through the lobby
🛗 Elevators that occasionally choose their own destination
🌞 Windows carefully angled to maximize dramatic sunsets
☕ A rooftop café designed specifically for philosophical architects

While most buildings simply stand still, the Banana Tower gently sways with the wind — a reminder that architecture, like life, should never be completely stable.
