The Floating Banana Tower 🍌

Our most ambitious project began with a simple question: "What if gravity was optional?"

The Floating Banana Tower is a 72-floor experimental skyscraper inspired by the natural elegance of a slightly overripe banana. Instead of straight lines, the tower curves toward the sun, creating one of the most unusual skylines ever imagined.

Inside the tower you will find vertical gardens, philosophical rooftop cafés, and elevators that sometimes choose their own destination.
